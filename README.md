# Johann’s Bizarre Adventure: ODIN

**Johann’s Bizarre Adventure** is a choice-based text adventure developed in Rust, set during the onset of World War II. You take on the role of Johann Joestar, a fourteen-year-old prisoner in rural Germany who must navigate a lethal conspiracy using the newly awakened power of his Stand.

---

## The Story
It is September 1939. You have spent three weeks in a cold German cell, watched closely by a guard named Hans. Tonight, your heritage awakens. Whether you walk out of those stone walls or fall victim to a temporal void depends entirely on your intuition and the allies you choose to trust.

---

## Stand Profiles

### **ODIN**
*   **User**: Johann Joestar
*   **Puppet Master**: By whispering the name of a target, Johann can attach ethereal threads to their soul, granting him absolute control over their physical actions.
*   **All-Seeing Eye**: ODIN possesses the ability to detect hidden glimmers of light and invisible Stand users that are otherwise imperceptible to humans.

### **LOKI**
*   **User**: Frank Zepelli
*   **Physical Manipulation**: Loki allows its user to alter the fundamental properties of any non-living matter it touches. This includes changing the size, shape, and temperature of objects—such as turning iron bars into liquid heat or evaporating shackles into steam.

---

## Features
*   **Branching Narrative**: Your decisions lead to multiple outcomes, ranging from a successful escape to a bizarre encounter with a neck-snapping Julius Caesar.
*   **State Machine Logic**: The game engine is built using a robust Rust architecture to ensure seamless scene transitions and logic handling.

## How to Play
1.  **Download**: Visit the  section of this repository and download the latest executable.
2.  **Run**: Launch the file. If Windows displays a "Protected Your PC" warning, click **More Info** followed by **Run Anyway**.
3.  **Interact**: Read the prompts and type the number corresponding to your choice, then press **Enter** to proceed.
